Advanced Research Projects

  1. Dynamic Hybrid Machine Learning Forecasting Mechanism for Time-Series Data
    Designed a novel hybrid model that dynamically adjusts the contribution of individual learning models to improve prediction accuracy over large-scale, time-dependent datasets.
  2. Reinforcement Learning and Lyapunov-Based Controller for Microgrid Stability
    Developed a control strategy combining Lyapunov stability theory with reinforcement learning to ensure adaptive stability in fluctuating microgrid environments.
  3. Adaptive Model Predictive Control (MPC) for Industrial Load Shedding
    Implemented a data-driven MPC approach that enables optimal and responsive load shedding in industrial sectors based on real-time conditions.
  4. Adaptive Hybrid Controller for Modular Multilevel Converter in Solid-State Transformer Applications
    Proposed an adaptive hybrid control technique for precise and robust operation of MMCs in advanced solid-state transformer architectures.
  5. Adaptive and Intelligent Power System Architecture
    Proposed a new swarm-based adaptive architecture for future power systems that integrates decentralized intelligence and real-time clustering.
  6. AI Guardian: Preventing Cascading Failures in Power Grids
    Introduced the concept of an “AI Guardian” to detect instability patterns and apply control actions in advance to prevent blackouts and cascading failures.
  7. Adaptive Heun-Based Controller for MMC Converters
    Developed a numerically stable and efficient adaptive controller based on Heun's method for control of modular multilevel converters.
