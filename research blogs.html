🔌 Grid-Forming Inverters and Spain Blackout – April 2025

April 28, 2025

In April 2025, a large blackout in Spain brought attention to the role of grid-forming inverters. These devices can generate their own voltage reference and help maintain grid stability without depending on synchronous machines, making them essential in renewable-heavy systems.
